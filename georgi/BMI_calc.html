What is Body Mass Index (BMI)?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation that estimates body fat based on a person's height and weight. The formula used is BMI = weight (kg) / height (mÂ²).

While BMI is a general tool to assess weight status, it's important to note that it doesn't distinguish between muscle and fat mass. Therefore, it may not be accurate for everyone, particularly muscle builders, long-distance athletes, pregnant women, the elderly, or young children. BMI is most applicable to adults aged 14-65 years.
